ABOUT

Public Print is a web-app development (software) project for broadcasting news from around the world in various field. This is in need that today people need more accurate and proper information. It is also used as a source for their knowledge and work. Here we do not only show the breaking news but also historical perspective on any topic, more about a culture, place, people, or time period information. Adding social media to this idea where we will open a new platform for every people where they can post their news from different background.
